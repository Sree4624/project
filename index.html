About

Welcome to our photography studio, where moments are transformed into timeless memories. Established in 1998, we have been capturing life’s most meaningful occasions with creativity, precision, and passion for over two decades. Our studio is led by Shaji Daylight, a highly experienced photographer with more than 30 years in the field. His dedication to the art of photography and keen eye for detail have earned him recognition as a two-time Best Photography Award winner. His expertise ensures that every frame tells a story and every image reflects genuine emotion. From weddings and portraits to special events and creative shoots, we focus on delivering high-quality, visually compelling photographs that our clients can cherish forever. Our commitment to professionalism, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in photography since 1998. We don’t just take photos — we capture memories that last a lifetime.

Founder
Shaji Daylight

Founder & Owner Behind our lens stands Shaji Daylight, the founder and owner of the studio, whose journey in photography spans over three decades. What began as a passion has evolved into a lifelong pursuit of capturing moments that speak beyond words. With more than 30 years of hands-on experience, he has developed a distinctive style defined by precision, creativity, and emotion. His work has been honored with the prestigious Best Photography Award on two occasions — a reflection of his unwavering commitment to excellence. Since establishing the studio in 1998, Shaji Daylight has not only built a trusted name in photography but also shaped countless memories into timeless visual stories. Every frame he captures carries depth, meaning, and authenticity. For him, photography is more than a profession — it is an art of preserving emotions, celebrating life, and turning fleeting moments into lasting memories.
